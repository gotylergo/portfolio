Tyler:

Web developer extrordonaire

Who are you?

Tyler is a talented web developer, music artist, unconventional geek, and neat freak. He's a well rounded professional with a great work ethic and he loves a challenge that he can really sink his gemini teeth into (that's his intellectually inclined sun sign, by the way).

Where are you from?

Hailing from the beautiful mid-western state of Ohio (specifically Cleveland), Tyler found his way to Los Angeles, California in his early 20s where he started fresh with a new home, new job, and new friends. After enjoying the weather and spending his free time singing, writing, and recording songs and vlogs on YouTube (a hobby and dream that he one day hopes to make a career), he decided it was time to see the world. Such began his journey to Australia, where he plans to make stunning websites, make catchy new pop songs, and see lots of Kangaroos!

What's important to you?

"Be the change you wish to see in the world."

One of my favorite quotes (a cheesy one liner with a quite disputed originator) describes one of my missions in life. I strive to be an example of at least 3 "-ions," which are: compassion, innovation, and passion. What I love about web design is my ability to express all three of these. I get to be compassionate towards someone's needs while expressing their vision, I get to excercise innovation in intelligently designed and planned websites and online applications, and I get to excercise my own passion to make the web, and the world, a more user friendly place to live.

It's a pleasure to meet you!
